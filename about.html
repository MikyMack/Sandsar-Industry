our process

Efficient & Sustainable Biodiesel Production Process

Our streamlined biodiesel production process ensures high efficiency and sustainability. From raw material processing to refining and distribution, we deliver high-quality biofuel while promoting a greener future.

01

Raw Material Collection & Processing

We collect and filter used cooking oil, vegetable oils, and animal fats to ensure a clean production process.

02

Biodiesel Production & Refining

The raw materials undergo transesterification, converting them into high-quality biodiesel, which is then refined and tested. .

03

Distribution & Sustainability

The final product is supplied for transportation and industrial use, promoting a cleaner and greener future.
